✅ 8 QUALITY METRICS

1. FAITHFULNESS (35% weight)
   - Is answer supported by retrieved documents?
   - Prevents hallucinations
   - Scoring: 1.0 (fully supported) to 0.0 (contradicted)

2. RELEVANCE (30% weight)
   - Does answer address the user's query?
   - Ensures on-topic responses
   - Based on keyword overlap and semantic similarity

3. PRECISION (20% weight)
   - What % of retrieved documents are relevant?
   - Formula: Correct retrieved / Total retrieved
   - Measures retrieval accuracy

4. RECALL (included in calculation)
   - What % of relevant documents were retrieved?
   - Formula: Correct retrieved / Total correct
   - Measures retrieval completeness

5. F1 SCORE (harmonic mean)
   - Balanced metric between precision and recall
   - Formula: 2 * (Precision * Recall) / (Precision + Recall)

6. CONSISTENCY (15% weight)
   - Do multiple similar queries give consistent answers?
   - Ensures stable system behavior

7. LATENCY (performance)
   - How fast are responses generated?
   - Normalized: 1.0 at 0s, 0.0 at 10s

8. COST (performance)
   - What is API cost per query?
   - Normalized: 1.0 at $0, 0.0 at $1.0
   - Important with FASE 18 optimization
